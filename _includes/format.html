IAA Workshop Format

Presentations

The workshop will include theory of fundamental concepts of identity, authentication, and authorisation, as well as federation. Examples of where and how federation is currently impacting and benefiting higher education around the world and in South Africa will also be discussed.
Check out the draft program.

Discussions & Hands-on Activities

There will be plenty of opportunity for hands-on practice throughout the event. Time will be made available to discuss challenges, solutions, and opportunities with colleagues in small groups as well as the bigger group. We also hope to define some terms of reference for keeping the conversations going and for further developing a community of practice after this event.

Networking Opportunities

Funding through RCCPII allows the opportunity to invite up to two nominated individuals from each public university in South Africa to stay stay over at Emperor's Palace Peermont Metcourt Hotel durint the event. We encourage you to make use of the opportunity to meet others and build your own network.
