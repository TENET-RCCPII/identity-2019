Accommodation Options for the Blended Learning SIG Meeting

Accommodation has been arranged at a special rate at the Peermont Metcourt Hotel. Please contact us to assist with your booking should you be responsible for funding your own accommodation.

If you have been nominated by your institution to attend, please indicate this on your registration form. Accommodation for up to three nominees per institution will be funded by the RCCPII grant. For more information, please get in touch with the organisers.
