  • Who Should Attend?

    IAA-2019 is targeted at application engineers and community managers with technical background in higher education in South Africa. Staff members from the 26 public universities are specifically encouraged to participate in the event.

  • Who is organizing IAA-2019?

    The workshop is funded by the Rural Campuses Connection Project II (RCCPII). The event is co-organise by representatives from the TENET (Guy Halse) and the RCCPII Capacity Development Initiative (Anelda van der Walt).

  • When and where will it be held?

    IAA-2019 will be held from 6 - 8 March, 2019 at Emperor's Palace Convention Centre, Johannesburg, South Africa.
